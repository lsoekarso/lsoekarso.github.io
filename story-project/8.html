She was surprised to find the cottage-door standing open, and when she went into the room, she had such a strange feeling that she said to herself:

'Oh dear! how uneasy I feel today, and at other times I like being with grandmother so much.' She called out: 'Good morning,'

but received no answer; so she went to the bed and drew back the curtains. There lay her grandmother with her cap pulled far over her face, and looking very strange.
